Sensing & Intuition

Thinking & Feeling

Thinking people are objecive and they make decision based on facts whereas
Feeling people are subjective and they make decision based on principles and value

Judging & Perceiving

Judgers approach life in a structured, organized and carefully calibrated way and Perceivers approach life in a freewheeling, spontaneous way, preferring to keep their options open than make a clear plan of action
